What is ?

ZINA is a custom and robust solution platform, developed within in LAT, and designed to simplify the way we work, allowing resources optimization, such as time and money, by providing automation and value creation to many of the operative activities performed by our sales, logistics, delivery, financial and other areas at Nokia.​

Our development team is a full-service software development group of doers. Experts, inside Nokia, that create high-value web and mobile applications, using Agile and DevOps methodologies and cutting-edge technologies.
